Hunt'n'Gather

Artificial Killing Machine is an installation
by Jonathan Moore in 2015 that shoots
toy guns every time a death occurs
due to US Drone strikes.
Can't Help Myself is a physical
computing installation featuring
a robotic arm programmed
to endlessly scrape red fluid
to stay within a programmed area
by Sun Yuan and Peng Yu in 2016.
Wake Up Machine by Simone Giertz
is an Arduino powered alarm clock that
slaps the user awake using a rubber arm.
