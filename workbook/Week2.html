This is a 'Crazy 8's' ideation task we did in our week 2 class, where we created 8 wireframes in 8 minutes to generate ideas quickly. This was an effective task for getting rid of 'plain' and 'boring' ideas and forcing us to think outside the box during the limited time frame.
Upon further development, this is a rough sketch of my overal website design, based on one of my wireframes from my Crazy 8s. I eventually chose this design as my final design.
This is another sketch of of a potential website design idea that involves a railway map style, with each stop being a 'week' of the semester. I will incorporate this idea into my navigation menu, but will not being using this as my final design.
